# Prohibited Activities

BlindPay maintains strict policies to ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements, protect our users, and maintain the integrity of our platform.

## Prohibited Business Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Adult Content & Sexually-Oriented Services** | Businesses offering or facilitating sexually explicit, obscene, or adult-oriented content or services |
| **Drugs, Controlled Substances, Alcohol & Pseudo-Pharmaceuticals** | Entities involved in the sale, distribution, or facilitation of illegal, controlled, or unregulated substances |
| **Weapons, Ammunition & Explosives** | Entities trading or dealing in weapons or weapon-related products |
| **Gambling, Betting & Games of Chance (Unlawful)** | Any business involving unlicensed or prohibited games of chance or gambling-related services |
| **Financial Crime-Linked Businesses & Fraudulent Models** | Business models or operations indicative of financial crimes or fraud schemes |
| **Hate, Violence, Terrorism & Discriminatory Activity** | Any activity promoting or enabling harm, hate, or exploitation |
| **Illegal or Harmful Activities** | Businesses engaging in or promoting unlawful or harmful behavior |
| **Sanctions, Legal, and Regulatory Violations** | Entities or persons in breach of international or local laws, including financial and export regulations |
| **Identity Fraud, Anonymity Misuse & Shell Structures** | Entities masking ownership or misrepresenting identity |
| **High-Risk Lending & Interest-Based Financial Services** | Predatory or non-compliant financial products |
| **Governmental, Diplomatic & Political Entities** | Due to complexity and enhanced risk profiles |
| **Data Misuse & Consumer Privacy Violations** | Businesses that compromise customer data or privacy |
| **Intellectual Property Infringement & Counterfeit Goods** | Entities selling or enabling IP-infringing or fake goods |

## Consequences of Violation

If prohibited business activity is identified:

- **Account Review**: The account will be reviewed and subject to suspension or termination
- **Fund Freezing**: Funds may be frozen pending investigation
- **Legal Notification**: Relevant partners and authorities may be notified in accordance with legal and contractual obligations

## Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any prohibited activities on the platform, please report them immediately to [compliance@blindpay.com](mailto:compliance@blindpay.com).
